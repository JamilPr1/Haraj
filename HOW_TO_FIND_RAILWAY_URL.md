# How to Find Your Railway Live URL

## Quick Steps

1. **Go to Railway Dashboard:**
   - Visit [railway.app](https://railway.app)
   - Log in to your account

2. **Select Your Project:**
   - Click on your project: **Haraj** (or whatever you named it)

3. **Find the URL:**
   - Look for the **"Settings"** tab
   - Or check the **"Deployments"** tab
   - The URL will be shown as:
     - `https://[your-project-name].railway.app`
     - Or a custom domain if you set one up

4. **Alternative:**
   - Click on the **"Deployments"** tab
   - Click on the latest deployment
   - The URL will be shown in the deployment details

## Common Railway URL Format

Railway URLs typically look like:
- `https://haraj-production.up.railway.app`
- `https://haraj-production.railway.app`
- Or a custom domain you configured
